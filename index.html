Starting up
🛠️ What should I do right now?
  • 1
    Turn off your PC and unplug it from the wall — safety first!
  • 2
    Open the side panel of your desktop, or find the air vents on your laptop.
  • 3
    Use a can of compressed air to blow out dust from the fans, vents, and heatsinks. Hold the can about 10 cm away.
  • 4
    Wipe down the vents with a dry cloth to remove any leftover dust from the surface.
  • 5
    Plug it back in and power on. Check this page again — the reading should drop within a few minutes.
