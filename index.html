Starting up
🔧 What should I do right now?
  • 1
    Turn off your PC and unplug it from the wall — safety first!
  • 2
    Open the side panel of your desktop, or find the air vents on your laptop.
  • 3
    Use a can of compressed air to blow out dust from the fans, vents, and heatsinks. Hold it about 10 cm away.
  • 4
    Wipe down the vents with a dry cloth to remove remaining surface dust.
  • 5
    Plug back in and power on. Check this page — readings should improve within minutes.
